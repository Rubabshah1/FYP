\section{Learning Reflection}
This project served as a significant technical milestone for the team, as it was our first time being exposed to and building an \textbf{Agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)} pipeline. We gained extensive hands-on experience in several cutting-edge AI domains:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Agentic AI \& RAG:} We learned to implement a multi-agent system, including \textbf{Router Agents} to optimize computational overhead and \textbf{Self-RAG Critics} to verify facts.
    \item \textbf{Classification \& Confidence:} We developed a \textbf{Confidence Scoring Engine} that aggregates retrieval similarity and token probability to ensure response reliability.
    \item \textbf{Human-in-the-Loop:} We gained insights into designing \textbf{escalation workflows} where the AI flags low-confidence queries for human intervention.
\end{itemize}

\section{Project Reflection}
The project successfully moved Alkhidmat Foundation toward an automated, multilingual communication system.
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Proposed vs. Achieved:} While the \textbf{Agentic RAG engine} and \textbf{Staff Dashboard} are functional, certain operational features were affected by external constraints, such as the Agentic RAG has not been integrated into Whatsapp yet.
    \item \textbf{Industry Partner Constraints:} The \textbf{Human Escalation} and real-time handoff have not been fully completed as intended due to delays in receiving necessary data and support from the \textbf{industry partner}.
    \item \textbf{Design Decisions:} We prioritized core domains like \textbf{Healthcare and Donation} to ensure a stable MVP, moving broader domain coverage to future work.
\end{itemize}

\section{Process Reflection}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{What Worked Well:} The \textbf{modular five-layer architecture} made the system highly maintainable and allowed for parallel development across the team.
    \item \textbf{Improvements:} Current end-to-end \textbf{latency} ranges from 30 to 60 seconds. Future iterations must focus on \textbf{embedding caches} and pipeline optimization to bring response times under 10 seconds.
\end{itemize}
